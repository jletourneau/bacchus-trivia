Ready to play live?

Bacchus holds trivia every Tuesday night at 7 PM at 33 Second Street in downtown Troy.

It’s always free to play, and there is no hard cap on team size (though keeping it under eight or so people is appreciated). The team leading at halftime receives a free round of up to four drinks, and the winning team at the end of the night receives a $25 gift card to Bacchus, redeemable on non-trivia nights.

Use the stairs leading down to the bar area to the left of the main building entrance at 33 Second Street. Free off-street evening parking is available two blocks west in the garage at the corner of River and State Street.

For updates from Bacchus, check out their page on Facebook.
